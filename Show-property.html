Casa Simoes - Candolim Villa in Goa

Spread across an acre and a half of lush green landscape, Casa Simoes is a beautiful property with an unmissable old world charm. The interiors echo the remnants of the gorgeous Portuguese architecture, while the cosy exteriors make the property an ideal group getaway. Merely minutes away from the beach, while here, guests can relax on the lawn hammock, enjoy their favourite drinks in the open air deck overlooking the iconic Goan sunset, or simply enjoy a splash in the pool. For a comfortable and secure stay, please take the time to read the Home Truths, House Rules and Policies thoroughly.

Villa Location

Known for its pristine touch and variety of amusement options around, Candolim is a small town located close to Baga, located on the shores of Arabian Sea. The white sandy beach of Candolim attracts a large number of tourists to enjoy various water activities like parasailing, bump rides, catamaran sailing, banana rides, windsurfing and more. So while youre enjoying your peaceful stay at this property, here are a few nearby places and activities we recommend to make your stay even more memorable.
- Experience the thrill of diving 
- Dine in a variety of fine dining and beach shacks
- Go for shopping in local markets 
- Visit Kegdole Beach and The Cabo Palace

Getting Around:
- Distance from Dabolim Airport - 45 km.
- Distance from Thivim Railway Station - 20 km.
- Distance from Candolim Beach Bus Stand - 1 km.
- Distance from Candolim Market - 1.4 km.

Casa Simoes is special because of its:
- Convenient location 
- Beautifully landscaped across 1.5 acres

We care for your absolute comfort! To that effect, we provide:
- Inverter, AC, WiFi, washing machine 
- Wardrobe, hangers
- Geysers, towels, toiletries
- Medical kit, mosquito repellent
- Barbecue kit 
- Secured parking space for up to 2 cars

The Space:

BEDROOMS
- There are 3 bedrooms - 2 on the first floor of the main villa and 1 in the outhouse, on the ground floor. 
- AC, WiFi, yoga mats and attached bathrooms are offered in all rooms.
- The rooms in the main villa have attached balconies with seating and the room in the outhouse has an attached patio, all offering picturesque garden views. 
- 1 room in the main villa has a twin bed and the other 2 rooms have king-sized beds. 

BATHROOMS
- There are 3 bathrooms in the villa - all attached.
- All bathrooms have geysers, towels and basic toiletries.

COMMON AREAS
- There is an expansive living room equipped with several modern amenities, including free Wi-Fi access, a television and music system. 
- The dining room is a part of the living room and offers comfortable seating for up to 6 people. 
- There is a huge landscaped lawn with a gazebo and a swimming pool, 12 ft. by 6 ft. in size and 4.5 ft. in depth, with pool loungers.

KITCHEN
- Guests may use the kitchen to prepare their own meals at no additional cost. 
- It is equipped with a microwave, gas stove, oven, refrigerator, water purifier, toaster and a mixer. 
- Crockery and cutlery are available.

FOOD
- All meals can be provided in-house at an additional cost, however, guests will have to pre-order their meals for the entire duration of the stay. 
- Only lunch is available at Rs. 2000 per day. 
- Lunch and dinner is provided at an additional cost of Rs. 3500 per day. 
- Breakfast, lunch and dinner is available Rs. 5500 per day. 
- The cost only includes the cooking charges, ingredients will be charged as per actuals. 

GUEST ACCESS
The guests have access to the entire villa. In response to the COVID-19 pandemic, guests may have restricted access to any shared amenities and swimming pool.

INTERACTION WITH GUESTS
The host is not present at the property but is always accessible via call or message. A trained and responsible caretaker is present at the property to resolve any concerns and help you have a perfect stay. The staff will be available only till 01:00 PM at the property.

STAFF ACCOMMODATION
We can provide accommodation for 1 staff member at no additional cost. Additional 2 male or 2 female staff members can be accommodated at Rs. 2000 per person, per day, excluding meal charges. Breakfast can be included at an additional cost of Rs. 400 per person, per day. 

ACTIVITIES
Guests can venture out and take a walk on the beach that is a few minutes away. Within the property, guests can enjoy a swim or play indoor games like cards and ludo. Guests can take a peaceful walk in the lawn or relax in the hammock. They can relish a delicious barbeque meal. A tent can also be arranged on request. 

ADDITIONAL COSTS
- Barbecue kit is available at no additional cost, however, coal will be charged as per actuals. 
- Tents can be arranged at an additional cost of Rs. 2000 per day. 
- Prices may vary subject to availability and peak season rates.

HOME TRUTHS
While we do promise you a welcoming home and a great stay, here are a few things wed like to mention so as to ensure there are no unpleasant surprises later!
-  The staff will be available only till 01:00 PM at the property.
- The meal plan will be applicable for the entire duration of the stay.
- There are 2 friendly dogs residing in the property. 
- In case of a power outage, the inverter can support all lights and fans for up to 4-6 hours. 
- This property has to be booked for a minimum period of 3 days. 
- WiFi, although offered, is subject to network availability at any given time.
- All mobile networks work fairly well here.

HOUSE RULES
- Loud music is not allowed post 10 PM.
- Alcohol consumption is allowed in designated areas. 
- Smoking is allowed only outdoors.
- For long bookings, washing machine can be used. 
- Children should be supervised while they are in or around the pool. 
- One must wear swimming attire and shower before entering the pool. 
- Guests are earnestly requested to treat the home with care.
- All-male groups are not allowed. 

POLICIES
- The full payment must be made for your booking to be confirmed.
- A refundable deposit of Rs. 30,000 will be collected before check-in.
- The number of guests must not exceed the count mentioned at the time of booking.
- Only the guests who have been accounted for are allowed at the villa.
- All guests must be able to share valid ID proofs when asked. This could be at the time of booking, and also upon arrival.
- All foreign nationals must be able to share their passport and visa details prior to their stay.
- Any damage to the property caused by the guest will be charged as per the actual cost of repair or replacement.
- Any illegal activity - including but not limited to prostitution and the use of narcotics - is strictly not permitted.
- Any commercial activity is strictly not permitted.
- This entire amount is refunded within two working days, provided the total number of guests does not increase and the check-out time is adhered to. 
- For long weekends and peak dates, we are unable to offer a refund. For all other dates, if you must cancel your booking, we offer a full refund if cancellation is done 45 days prior to the check-in. We offer a 50% refund if cancellation is done 30-45 days prior to the check-in. we offer a 25% refund if cancellation is done 15-30 days prior to the check-in. We are unable to offer a refund if the cancellation is made 15 days prior to your stay.
- In case of any delays or other grievances, guests can reach out to guestsupport@stayvista.com for assistance.

We look forward to hosting you soon. As soon as you book, our team will get in touch to help you with any additional information that you may require, and we will be at your service until check-out.
